Our Story: A Passion for Thread

Founded in 1995 by Mohini Ganesh, MG's Tailoring began with a simple mission: to combine traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary design. What started in a small workshop has grown into one of Mumbai's most trusted names for bespoke tailoring, all while holding onto the core values of quality, precision, and personal service.

About MG's Tailoring

Crafting bespoke garments with a passion for quality and a commitment to the perfect fit. Serving Mumbai with pride since 2005.
